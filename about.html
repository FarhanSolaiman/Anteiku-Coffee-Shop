It’s that simple. We've built our business around our love of great, light-roasted coffee. Year round we bring you the best in-season beans from around the world and roast them right here in Caswynn.

OWNER & FOUNDER

Mr. Yoshimura


Born and raised in Tokyo, Japan spent seven years working in coffee in Tokyo City. In 2013, he headed to the East to open a modern café and roastery built on a healthy obsession with great tasting coffee and welcoming service. He's happy for his business, roasting and serving his favorite coffees in Caswynn.
